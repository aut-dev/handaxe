# STORYBOARD: Episode 1 — "Beans"

## A Day in the Life of Todd — Handaxe Studios

---

## Reading This Document

Each panel is one **generation unit** — a single AI video generation pass. Long shots from the teleplay have been split into sub-panels (2A/2B, etc.) to stay within the ~10-12 second coherence window per generation. The panels are designed to cut together seamlessly.

**Panel format:**
- **Frame** — what the camera sees, described as a composition
- **Action** — what happens within the shot
- **Audio** — dialogue, SFX, ambient
- **Expression** — Todd's face/body language (keyed to the character sheet)
- **Duration** — target length for this generation
- **Generation prompt** — ready-to-use prompt combining character, location, and action details
- **Notes** — continuity flags, risk level, technical considerations

Same composition as Panel 9 — Todd facing camera, stove behind him. He's still leaning on the counter but his energy has shifted. The formal hosting cadence relaxes. He pushes his hair out of his eyes. Behind him, a thin wisp of smoke begins to rise from the pan — barely visible at first.

Then he recovers into a gentler version of **The Host**: "this is the journey" is said with real warmth, real vulnerability, quickly papered over with a nod and a reset.

**Duration:** 12 seconds

**Generation prompt:**
> Semi-stylized painterly realism, 4:5 aspect ratio. Interior galley kitchen, slightly low angle from counter height.
